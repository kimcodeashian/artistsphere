Welcome To ArtistSphere!

What Is ArtistSphere?

ArtistSphere is a web application created to help you discover new artists and songs based on artists that you already know and love! It is built using the Spotify API, and draws from it's entire library, giving access to millions of songs and artists.

How Does It Work?

1. Enter an artist in the search bar below

2. ArtistSphere will reveal 10-25 related artists along with their top 10 tracks!

3. Click the tracks to play music, or click an artist's name to make them the new search point.
